FreedomValues

What is FreedomValues?

FreedomValues is a political quiz specifically aimed at those aligned with classical liberalism, libertarianism, liberalism or other similar ideologies. This test will attempt to place you on 9 seperate axes representing varying opinions & differences within liberal/libertarian movements, through answering a series of questions.

There are questions in the test.

What is "Closest Match" in the results?

Along with obviously displaying your quiz results, the test will also attempt to match you with a libertarian school of thought, which is based off your results.
